Quick Start
Three steps to your reel
01
Write your script
Type your lines using the simple colon format. Each line prefixed with : becomes one animated text card in your video.
: Your first line here : Second line of text : Keep it short & punchy : One thought per line
02
Customise style
Choose a mood preset or mix manually — pick your effects, font, palette, shadow style, and background image. Hit Randomise to auto-generate a look.
Mood → Luxury / Hype / Cinematic FX → Color Grade / Duotone / Stamp Font → Bebas / Playfair / Orbitron… BG → Solid / Transparent / Photo
03
Generate & export
Click Generate to render a live preview on canvas. Set your frame rate (12–60 fps), then hit Download MP4 to save the final video file.
FPS → 12 / 24 / 30 / 60 Format → 9:16 Reel, 1:1 Square, 16:9 YouTube BG Mode → Colour or Transparent
Script Format
The colon format explained
Example script
:ajj kese hai app :mei bahut badhiya :kya scene hai yaar :sab sahi chal raha :let's go 🔥
→ Each : line = one animated scene
→ Lines render one at a time
→ Emojis are supported
✏️
Prefix every line with :Lines without a colon prefix are ignored. Keep each line short and punchy for the best visual impact.
⚡
One idea per lineThe engine renders each line as a standalone card with its own animation. Shorter lines = bigger, bolder text.
🎲
Use RandomiseHit the Randomise Effects button to auto-assign a different animation effect and style to every line in your script.
🔢
Live statsThe Stats panel shows you line count, word count, estimated video duration, and frame count at 24fps in real time.
What's inside
Full feature breakdown
⚡ FX Mix Mode lets you apply effects in Random, Single, or Rotating order across your lines.
🎨
Color Grade
Applies a retro colour grading treatment to each text card. Rich, warm tones that evoke analogue film.
◼
Flat Fill
Clean solid-colour fills with no effects. Maximum legibility for bold, graphic statements.
◑
Duotone
Two-tone colour treatment. Stark, high-contrast style popular in editorial design and posters.
🔤
Stamp
Distressed rubber-stamp look with slight bleed and texture. Great for raw, authentic aesthetics.
◻
Outline
Stroke-only text with transparent fill. Elegant and minimal — pairs perfectly with photo backgrounds.
⧉
Offset Print
Misaligned colour layers simulating a misregistered print. Retro, zine-inspired visual style.
Per-Letter Mode (NEW)
🔡
Split to Letters
Explodes each word into individual animating characters.
🌊
Stagger Entry
Each letter enters with a configurable delay (10–200ms) for wave effects.
🎨
Color Per Letter
Every character is assigned a separate palette colour for a rainbow typewriter effect.

Background Flash

🌊
Idle Breathe

Typography Controls
• Font size range: 18–130px
• Text tilt: 0–40°

• Color intensity: Flat / Soft / Medium / Rich / Deep
